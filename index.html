FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Below are answers to some common questions about the workshop.

Do I need prior experience in data analysis?

No. The workshop is practical and suitable for participants who want guided exposure to data cleaning, dashboards and AI-assisted reporting.

Will participants be guided on installing the required software?

Yes. Participants will be guided on how to access or install the required tools for the workshop. The main tools are Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Power BI, ChatGPT and Claude AI.

Which AI tools will be used during the workshop?

The main AI tools will be ChatGPT and Claude AI. We may also demonstrate how tools such as Google Gemini and Microsoft Copilot can support analysis, interpretation, dashboard planning and report writing.

How will the workshop be delivered?

The workshop will be delivered online via Microsoft Teams from 0900hrs to 1200hrs Central African Time, from 6 to 8 July 2026.

How much is the workshop fee?

The workshop fee is US$60.

Will participants receive datasets and examples?

Yes. The workshop is designed around practical examples, datasets and dashboard illustrations.

Will a certificate be provided?

Yes. A certificate of participation will be provided.

Can this training be customised for an organisation?

Yes. The content can be tailored for companies, NGOs, research teams, schools and other institutions.
